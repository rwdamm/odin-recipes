Mexican Chicken and Rice Casserole

A stick to your ribs budget recipie

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lbs - Chicken Thighs (skinned removed, boned and cut into bite size pieces)
  • 16 oz - Brown Rice
  • 15 oz can - Kidney Beans (undrained)
  • 15 oz can - Diced Tomatoes with Green Chiles
  • 1 - Bell Pepper (diced)
  • 1 - Yellow Onion (diced)
  • 8 oz - Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese
  • 1 - Taco Seasoning Packet
  • 30 oz - Water

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium high heat cook chicken 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add bell pepper, onions and cook another 2 minutes.
  3. Add remaining ingredients except the cheese and mix well.
  4. Transfer to a 9x13 casserole dish and cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in a 375ยบ oven for 1 hour or until all of the liquid has been absorbed.
  6. Mix in half the cheese, then top with remaining cheese and bake uncovered 5-7 minutes in a 400ยบ oven or until golden brown and bubbly.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes.

Enjoy
