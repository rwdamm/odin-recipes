Canola Oil Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

This is an oatmeal raisin cookie developed to use canola oil instead of solid fats

Ingredients

  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 4 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease cookie sheets.
  2. In a large bowl, mix canola oil, brown sugar, eggs and vanilla until well blended.
  3. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg; stir into the sugar mixture.
  4. Mix in the oats and raisins last. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto the prepared cookie sheet.
  5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven.
  6. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Enjoy
